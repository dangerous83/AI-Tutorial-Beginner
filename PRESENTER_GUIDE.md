**Host:** PMCC 4th Watch • **Presenter:** Bro. Alvin — Professional Graphic Designer & AI Specialist

## 📋 Slide-by-Slide Notes

### Front Page (Cover)
- **Presenter notes:** Have this screen up as people walk in — the terminal intro grabs attention. Greet everyone warmly. When ready, press START (or Enter). Do a quick sound check with the speaker icon.
- **Voice-over:** "Welcome, everyone, to AI Starter Lab — hosted by PMCC 4th Watch! I'm Bro. Alvin, a graphic designer and AI specialist. In the next two hours you won't just learn about AI... you'll actually create with it. Ready? Let's boot up!"
- **AI image prompt:** `futuristic glowing AI hologram interface, neon blue and gold light particles, dark tech background, church youth event energy, cinematic, ultra detailed`

### Slide 1 — Welcome to AI Starter Lab
- **Presenter notes:** Icebreaker: "Who here has ever used ChatGPT or an AI filter?" Count hands. Invite 4 volunteers to click each mission card. Emphasize: today is HANDS-ON, not a lecture.
- **Voice-over:** "Here's our mission for today. Four superpowers: creating images, making videos, designing ads, and building presentations — all with AI. No experience needed. If you can type a sentence, you can do this."
- **AI image prompt:** `four glowing holographic app icons floating above a teenager's open hand, neon blue interface, dark futuristic room, gold accents, 3D render`

### Slide 2 — What is AI?
- **Presenter notes:** Keep the definition simple: AI learns patterns from examples, like learning a song by hearing it many times. Click the reveal cards one by one — let students guess first. Myth-bust: AI doesn't "think," it predicts patterns.
- **Voice-over:** "So what exactly is AI? It's a computer system that learns patterns from millions of examples so it can write, draw, and talk like a human helper. And here's the twist — you already use AI every single day. Let's prove it."
- **AI image prompt:** `friendly glowing digital brain made of blue circuit lines with gold sparks, floating above a smartphone, dark background, clean tech illustration`

### Slide 3 — Why AI Matters for Students & Creators
- **Presenter notes:** Make it personal: "Who has a school project due soon? Who wants to start a YouTube channel or small business?" Connect each card to their answers. Pause after reading the closing quote.
- **Voice-over:** "Why should YOU care? Because AI is a study buddy that never sleeps, a creative superpower even if you can't draw, and one of the most in-demand skills for your future. Remember: AI won't replace people — but people who use AI will move faster than people who don't."
- **AI image prompt:** `diverse group of teenagers using futuristic holographic screens for study and design, neon blue and gold lighting, inspiring mood, cinematic wide shot`

### Slide 4 — How to Write a Powerful Prompt
- **Presenter notes:** THE most important slide of the day. Teach R-T-C-F slowly: Role, Task, Context, Format. Read the weak prompt vs. the powerful one; if internet is available, run both live in ChatGPT. Have the room chant "Role, Task, Context, Format!"
- **Voice-over:** "Talking to AI is a skill, and it has a formula: R-T-C-F. Give the AI a Role, a Task, some Context, and a Format. Watch the difference between 'make a poster' and a real power prompt. The better your instructions, the better your results — in AI and in life!"
- **AI image prompt:** `glowing command line terminal with golden text forming a rising staircase of words, dark blue tech background, symbolic of leveling up, digital art`

### Slide 5 — AI Image Generation
- **Presenter notes:** Demo time (5 min): open Bing/Copilot Image Creator and type the on-screen demo prompt. While it generates, walk the tools grid. If offline, show 2–3 pre-generated examples. Note most tools have free tiers.
- **Voice-over:** "Now the fun part — AI art. You type a description, and seconds later the AI paints it. This is called text-to-image. There are many tools, and several are free. Let's generate something live, right now."
- **AI image prompt:** `a digital paintbrush made of light painting a vivid fantasy landscape out of pixels, neon blue and gold palette, magical, ultra detailed`

### Slide 6 — Image Prompt Formula
- **Presenter notes:** Interactive: call 6 students up — each flips a card and reads it aloud. Then read the assembled full prompt. Optional: generate it live to prove the formula works.
- **Voice-over:** "Here's the secret recipe pros use: Subject, Setting, Style, Lighting, Mood, Quality. Six ingredients. Stack them together and your images go from random to breathtaking. Let's flip each card and build a full prompt together."
- **AI image prompt:** `six glowing ingredient vials on a futuristic lab table labeled with holographic icons, neon blue glass, gold liquid, dark sci-fi laboratory, 3D render`

### Slide 7 — AI Video Generation
- **Presenter notes:** Explain the two modes (text-to-video, image-to-video) with the big cards first. If possible, play one short pre-downloaded AI video clip. Set honest expectations: free tiers are limited and clips are short — but improving monthly.
- **Voice-over:** "If AI can paint a picture... can it film a movie? Yes! AI video works two ways: describe a scene with text, or upload a photo and watch it come alive. Tools like Sora, Runway, and Pika are rewriting what one creative person can do."
- **AI image prompt:** `holographic film clapperboard exploding into glowing video frames, robot hand holding it, neon blue and gold cinematic lighting, dark studio`

### Slide 8 — Video Prompt Formula
- **Presenter notes:** Highlight what's NEW vs. the image formula: **Action** (what moves) and **Camera** (how it's filmed). Teach 3 camera words: zoom, pan, low angle. Read the full prompt like a director announcing a shot.
- **Voice-over:** "Video prompts start like image prompts but add two magic ingredients: Action — what moves — and Camera — how we film it. Say it like a director: a rainy neon street, a robot with an umbrella walks toward the camera, slow dolly zoom. Lights, prompt, action!"
- **AI image prompt:** `futuristic director's chair and camera drone filming a glowing robot on a neon rainy street, gold lens flares, cinematic behind-the-scenes shot`

### Slide 9 — Creating Social Media Ads with AI
- **Presenter notes:** Use a relatable scenario: promoting a youth event, school club, or small online shop. Walk the 4 steps left to right. Emphasize: AI does the drafts, THEY provide taste and judgment. Mention the A/B testing pro tip.
- **Voice-over:** "Let's get practical: making an ad for social media. Old way: you'd need a copywriter, designer, and editor. New way: you plus AI. Idea, copy, visual, assemble — four steps, one afternoon, zero budget."
- **AI image prompt:** `smartphone displaying a vibrant social media ad, surrounded by floating holographic like and share icons, neon blue and gold, dark background, 3D render`

### Slide 10 — Hook + Visual + CTA
- **Presenter notes:** Flip the three cards one at a time. Mini-game: say a boring line ("We have an event Saturday") and challenge students to shout better hooks. Then show the example. This formula alone is gold for future content creators.
- **Voice-over:** "Every great ad in your feed follows one formula: Hook, Visual, C-T-A. The hook grabs you in two seconds. The visual makes you feel. The call-to-action tells you what to do next. Master these three and your posts stop getting scrolled past."
- **AI image prompt:** `three glowing holographic panels labeled hook, visual, CTA floating in a dark futuristic room, gold fishing hook icon glowing, neon blue interface`

### Slide 11 — Creating Presentations using GPT
- **Presenter notes:** School superpower slide. Walk the 4 steps, then read the Mega Prompt slowly — tell students to photograph it. Stress step 4: THEY are the editor-in-chief. If time allows, run the mega prompt live on a topic the audience picks.
- **Voice-over:** "Homework hack, unlocked: never start a presentation from a blank slide again. Ask GPT for an outline, then slide text, paste it into Canva or PowerPoint, and polish it with your own style. AI is your intern — you are the editor-in-chief."
- **AI image prompt:** `student at a futuristic desk while a friendly AI hologram assembles presentation slides in the air, neon blue interface, gold highlights, inspiring`

### Slide 12 — Turning Presentation into PDF
- **Presenter notes:** Quick, practical slide (2–3 min). Do a 30-second live demo of Print → Save as PDF — that trick works in ANY app. Bottom row explains WHY PDF matters: sharing, printing, locking the design.
- **Voice-over:** "Final step of any project: saving it as a PDF. Why PDF? It opens on every device, keeps your fonts and layout perfect, and nobody can accidentally edit it. Easiest universal trick: Print, then choose Save as PDF. Works everywhere!"
- **AI image prompt:** `glowing document icon transforming into a shielded PDF file, blue data particles flowing, gold lock symbol, minimal dark tech illustration`

### Slide 13 — Responsible AI Use
- **Presenter notes:** Serious tone shift — slow down. Walk the DO list, then the DON'T list. Discuss deepfakes: "If you saw a video of a friend saying something bad, could you be sure it's real?" Emphasize honesty about AI help and never sharing private info. Connect to integrity and character.
- **Voice-over:** "With great power comes great responsibility. AI can make you faster — it should never make you dishonest. Double-check facts, credit AI when it helps, never fake real people, and keep private things private. The tool is powerful; YOUR character decides if it's used well."
- **AI image prompt:** `glowing golden shield protecting a digital heart made of circuits, dark background with blue data streams, symbolic, dramatic lighting, digital art`

### Slide 14 — Mini Quiz
- **Presenter notes:** Energy break! Split the room into 2 teams. Read each question, let teams shout answers, then hit REVEAL (plays a success sound). Keep score on paper. Small prizes (candy) work wonders. ~5 minutes.
- **Voice-over:** "Checkpoint time! Let's see what stuck. Four questions, two teams, bragging rights on the line. Shout your answers before I hit reveal!"
- **AI image prompt:** `futuristic game show stage with glowing question mark hologram, two podiums, neon blue and gold spotlights, exciting atmosphere, 3D render`

### Slide 15 — Prompt Challenge
- **Presenter notes:** Activity (~10 min incl. sharing). Demo the Prompt Builder: change dropdowns, hit RANDOMIZE a few times, then COPY. Students write their own prompt on paper/phone using the formula. Start the 3-minute timer. Afterwards, 3–4 volunteers read theirs aloud; class votes for the best.
- **Voice-over:** "Time to compete! Use the formula — subject, setting, style, lighting, mood — and build the most creative image prompt you can. You have three minutes on the clock. The best prompt wins glory... and maybe candy. Ready? Go!"
- **AI image prompt:** `glowing countdown timer hologram above excited students writing, neon blue sparks, gold trophy in background, competitive fun atmosphere, cinematic`

### Slide 16 — Final Activity
- **Presenter notes:** The big build (~20–25 min total). Teams of 2–4 pick ONE track — click through the four tabs to explain each mission. Start the 10-minute timer. Circulate and help. Reserve ~10 min for teams to present. This is the memory they take home.
- **Voice-over:** "Final mission! Team up, choose your track — image, video, ad, or presentation — and create something REAL using everything you learned today. Ten minutes on the clock. Then each team presents. Make me proud, creators!"
- **AI image prompt:** `four teams of young creators at glowing workstations, each station a different color hologram, mission control room vibes, neon blue and gold, wide shot`

### Slide 17 — Closing Message
- **Presenter notes:** Celebration + send-off. Read the certificate dramatically, then hit CELEBRATE (confetti + fanfare). Closing charge: "You came in as AI users, you leave as AI creators." Thank PMCC 4th Watch, take a group photo with this slide on screen, share resource links.
- **Voice-over:** "Congratulations! You are now officially graduates of the AI Starter Lab. You didn't just learn about AI today — you created with it. Use these powers with creativity, honesty, and heart. Now go make something amazing. The future is yours!"
- **AI image prompt:** `golden certificate with glowing seal floating amid celebration confetti, neon blue light rays, dark elegant background, triumphant mood, ultra detailed`
