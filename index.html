MODULE 04

How to Write a Powerful Prompt ✍️

A prompt is the instruction you give AI. Better instruction = better result. Use the R‑T‑C‑F formula:

R

Role

“Act as a graphic designer…”

+
T

Task

“…create 3 poster ideas…”

+
C

Context

“…for a youth church event…”

+
F

Format

“…as a bullet list.”

😐 Weak prompt

"make a poster"

🤩 Powerful prompt

"Act as a graphic designer. Create 3 poster concepts for a youth event called AI Starter Lab. Audience is ages 12–22. Give each concept a title, color palette, and slogan, as a bullet list."

MODULE 13

Responsible AI Use 🛡️

With great power comes great responsibility. The Creator's Code:

✅ DO

  • Use AI to learn faster and brainstorm ideas
  • Double‑check facts — AI can be confidently wrong
  • Be honest: say when AI helped you
  • Keep personal info private
  • Add your own voice — AI is the tool, you are the artist

⛔ DON'T

  • Copy AI answers as homework and call it yours
  • Create fake images of real people (deepfakes)
  • Share passwords, addresses, or private photos with AI
  • Believe everything AI says without checking
  • Use AI to bully, trick, or harm anyone
